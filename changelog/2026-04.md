## 2026-04-14 — Songwriting Craft Expansion

### Summary
Added four new shared guides focused on authentic songwriting psychology, lyric craft, vocal direction, and arrangement feel. Grounded in scholarly research (music cognition, songwriting pedagogy, AI perception studies) and community-tested techniques (Reddit, Facebook, YouTube — January–April 2026). No artist names appear in any guide — all techniques are described as transferable, genre-agnostic principles.

### Research Sources
- Academic: Ayyildiz et al. (2025) on micro-timing; Kilchenmann & Senn (2016) on groove; Juslin BRECVEMA framework; Egermann & McAdams (2013) on emotional contagion; Werner (2021) on lyric register; Zhu et al. (ACL 2025) on AI lyric prosody; PLOS ONE (2024) narrative arc study of 12,280 songs; CMU (2026) creativity gap research; arXiv Turing test study
- Community: r/SunoAI, r/AIMusic, r/WeAreTheMusicMakers, r/Songwriting, Facebook Suno groups, HookGenius, Jack Righteous, AI Video Bootcamp, Robin Frederick songwriting pedagogy
- Artist technique analysis: Cross-artist study of authentic songwriting patterns, anonymized into 22 transferable principles

### Files Created

#### Shared (model-agnostic)
- `shared/songwriting-craft.md` (386 lines) — Psychology of authentic songwriting. Emotional architecture (verse-as-investigation, chorus-as-verdict, bridge-as-reframe). Narrative techniques (scene anchoring, temporal distance, dialogue as evidence). Vulnerability principle. Anti-generic checklist. Character Prompt Method for AI prompting.
- `shared/lyric-writing.md` (458 lines) — Syllable density and line length rules. Prosody alignment (stressed syllables on downbeats). Conversational vs. poetic register. Concrete imagery with AID method. Rhyme mechanics (near-rhyme for verses, perfect rhyme for hooks). Vernacular and dialect. Full anti-cliché reference table. 10-point lyric quality checklist.
- `shared/vocal-direction.md` (362 lines) — Character Prompt Method (describe a person in a moment, not a voice). Vocal Anchor system for locking vocal identity. Physical voice descriptors over gender tags. Dynamic contrast patterns. In-lyric performance tags. Spelling/stretching/vocal spice tricks. Pacing and delivery control. Common failure table with fixes.
- `shared/arrangement-feel.md` (329 lines) — Describe physics not genre. Space and silence as tools. Acoustic instruments as authenticity markers. Micro-timing and first-take feel (tight-but-non-zero groove). Dynamic arrangement across sections with behavior tags. Spatial prompting (room, mic, reverb, stereo). Exclusion prompting. Melodic choices (speech cadence, interval leaps, delayed resolution).
